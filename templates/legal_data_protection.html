At NamEngine LLC ("NamEngine," "we," "our," or "us"), we believe that choosing a name is one of life’s most meaningful decisions. The information you choose to share with us is personal, and we are committed to treating it with care, respect, and transparency.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the choices available to you when using our website, applications, and services (collectively, the "Services").

By using the Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Our Privacy Commitment

Your trust is important to us, and protecting your privacy is one of our highest priorities.

We never sell your personal information.

We do not share your personal information with any third party for their own marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes.

We share information only when it is necessary to operate, secure, maintain, or provide the NamEngine services you request. When we do, we work only with trusted service providers that are contractually required to protect your information and may use it only to perform services on our behalf.

We collect only the information reasonably necessary to personalize your experience, improve our Services, maintain security, and support our users.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the Services, we may collect the following categories of information.

Information You Choose to Provide

Examples include:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Account information
  • Profile information
  • Customer support communications
  • Feedback and survey responses
  • Payment-related information, processed by trusted third-party payment providers

For NamEngine naming experiences, you may also choose to provide information such as:

  • Naming preferences
  • Style preferences
  • Cultural or heritage preferences
  • Family preferences
  • Reactions to recommendations
  • Notes and comments
  • Other information you voluntarily provide to personalize recommendations

Providing this information is optional unless it is required for a specific feature.

Like most modern websites and applications, we automatically collect limited technical information that helps us operate, secure, and improve our Services.

This information may include:

  • IP address

We use your information to:

To generate personalized naming recommendations, NamEngine securely uses trusted artificial intelligence technology providers.

Information is shared only as necessary to provide the recommendations you request and is handled in accordance with contractual privacy and security obligations.

We do not sell your personal information to AI providers, and they may not use your information for their own marketing or commercial purposes.

We never sell your personal information.

We do not share your personal information with any third party for their own marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes.

These providers may access information only to perform services on our behalf and are contractually required to protect your information.

We may also disclose information when required by law or when necessary to protect the rights, safety, or security of NamEngine, our users, or others.

8. Protecting Your Information

Protecting your information is a priority for us.

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

While no online service can guarantee absolute security, we continually work to maintain appropriate safeguards for the information entrusted to us.
