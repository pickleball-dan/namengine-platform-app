# Namengine Vision

Namengine will become the world's most trusted platform for discovering meaningful names.

## What We Are Building

A shared identity-discovery platform that helps people name children, pets, businesses, products, characters, places, and future categories without rebuilding the core experience each time.

## The Experience

Users should feel understood, surprised, and confident. They should remember the name they discovered and why it fits—not the AI model that produced it.

## The Platform

Namengine will combine:

- structured intake and the Feelings Scale
- taste interpretation
- candidate generation
- validation and cultural care
- clear explanations
- reactions and refinement
- comparison and decision support
- keepsakes and shareable outcomes
- provider routing and quality learning

## The Long-Term Outcome

Namengine becomes the place people turn when identity matters and the decision deserves more than a list.
