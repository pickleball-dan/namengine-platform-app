# Strategy

## Focus

Ship Namengine Baby before expanding the platform or Mission Control.

## How We Win

1. Understand taste better than generic AI tools.
2. Produce fewer, stronger recommendations.
3. Explain why each name fits.
4. Validate practical and cultural risks.
5. Learn from reactions and chosen names.
6. Route work to the best available model without exposing provider complexity to users.

## Sequence

1. Baby
2. Pet
3. Business
4. Product
5. Character
6. Additional identity categories

A new vertical launches only after the shared platform supports its intake, generation, validation, refinement, comparison, and decision experience.

## Competitive Advantage

Our advantage is not access to an AI model. It is the system around the models:

- Feelings Scale
- taste interpretation
- vertical expertise
- validation
- explanation quality
- reaction learning
- evaluation fixtures
- provider performance data

## Operating Constraint

Infrastructure and internal tools receive attention only when they remove a direct shipping or learning bottleneck.
