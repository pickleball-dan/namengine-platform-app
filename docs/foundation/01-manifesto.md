# Namengine Manifesto

Namengine helps people make meaningful identity decisions with confidence.

A name carries emotion, culture, expectation, and story. It deserves more than a random list.

We exist to create discovery: the moment someone finds a name they would not have considered, understands why it fits, and feels certain enough to act.

We do not optimize for volume. We optimize for relevance, trust, and delight.

Every recommendation must earn its place.
